# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. They may
remove comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this
code.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer directly: https://github.com/eyupk3

If you need a repository-specific report path, use:
https://github.com/eyupk3/awesome-expo-react-native-dx/issues/new/choose

All reports will be reviewed and handled fairly and promptly.
